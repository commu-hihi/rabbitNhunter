Rules

  • YOU are the hunter. you must catch the rabbit.
  • First, the rabbit takes turn.
  • The rabbit can move up to 7 spaces.
  • also, The rabbit can move 7 minus the number of spaces it has moved before
  • Alternately move forward and backward on the rabbit's turn.
  • And the hunter takes turn.
  • The hunter can shoot or block.
  • If you shoot, get chance to catch the rabbit.
  • If you block, restrict the rabbit's move.
  • When the hunter catches the rabbit, the hunter wins.
  • When the rabbit reach the goal, the rabbit wins.
